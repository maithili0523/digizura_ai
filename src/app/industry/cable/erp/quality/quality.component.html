Quality Management establishes a three-tier quality assurance framework that safeguards product integrity at every stage. From incoming material inspection through in-process monitoring to final certification, this comprehensive system ensures cables meet exact specifications and regulatory requirements.

Quality Management

Inward Quality Control

Inward Quality Control acts as the first line of defense against substandard materials entering production. Linked directly to Goods Receipt Notes (GRN), this process systematically inspects every material delivery against predefined specifications.

  • GRN-Based Inspection – Automated quality checks at receipt
  • Acceptance / Rejection – Pass/fail decisions with documentation
  • NCR Generation – Instant non-conformance tracking
  • Vendor Feedback – Supplier quality communication loop
Quality Management

In-Process Quality Control

In-Process Quality Control provides real-time quality monitoring throughout the manufacturing journey. At each production stage—drawing, stranding, insulation, and beyond—critical parameters are automatically captured and validated against specifications.

1
Stage-Wise Testing

Quality gates at each manufacturing phase

2
Parameter Capture

Automated measurement and documentation

3
Auto Block on Deviation

Instant halt when specs are not met

4
Corrective Actions

Systematic resolution and tracking

Live Demo Feature: Real-time quality monitoring dashboard showing live QC checks during active production runs with instant deviation alerts.

Final Quality & Test Certificate

Final Quality Control represents the culmination of the quality assurance process. Comprehensive testing protocols validate that finished cables meet all electrical, mechanical, and dimensional specifications. Test results are automatically captured and transformed into professional test certificates formatted to customer requirements.

Comprehensive electrical and mechanical validation using calibrated equipment against international standards

Test Result Capture

Auto Certificate Generation
