Master Data Management

The foundation of accurate, compliant, and integrated ERP operations.

Customer Master

  • Customer classification by industry and region
  • GST and statutory tax configuration
  • Credit limits and payment terms
  • Customer-specific pricing
  • Approval workflows for exceptions

Vendor Master

  • Vendor registration and compliance records
  • Raw material and service vendor classification
  • GST, TDS, and regulatory setup
  • Payment terms and credit conditions
  • Vendor performance tracking

Raw Material Master

  • Copper, PVC, XLPE, and packing materials
  • Units of measure and conversions
  • Quality parameters and tolerances
  • Storage rules and traceability
  • Scrap identification and handling

Finished Goods Master

  • Cable type, size, and voltage grade
  • Insulation and sheathing specifications
  • Drum, coil, and packing configuration
  • Testing and certification standards
  • Batch and serial traceability

Price Master

  • Customer-wise negotiated pricing
  • Product-wise base pricing
  • Validity periods and renewals
  • Margin thresholds and alerts
  • Approval-based price changes
