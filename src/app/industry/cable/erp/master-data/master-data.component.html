MASTER DATA MANAGEMENT

Finished Goods Master

Configuration Elements
  • Cable Type Configuration: Power, Control, Instrumentation, LT, HT, Solar and Special Purpose cables with full technical attributes.
  • Size & Specification Mapping: Core count, conductor material (Cu/Al), strand class, insulation, sheath, voltage grade, and current rating.
  • Drum / Coil Configuration: Drum size, flange type, packing length, gross weight, export packing and labeling standards.
  • Testing Standards Mapping: IS, IEC, BS, ASTM and customer-specific routine, type and acceptance tests.
  • Finished Goods Pricing: Base price with copper index linkage, insulation cost factor, overhead absorption and margin rules.
  • Quality Certificates: Automatic generation of test certificates, conformity reports and compliance documents.
  • Serialization & Traceability: Batch-wise, drum-wise and meter-wise tracking from production to dispatch.
Operational Controls
  • Revision Control: Versioning of cable designs and specifications.
  • Regulatory Compliance: RoHS, REACH, BIS, CE and customer audit readiness.
  • Export Documentation: HS Code, COO, packing list and shipping marks mapping.
  • AI-driven Recommendations: Suggest optimal cable configuration based on application and load conditions.
Business Impact

A well-structured Finished Goods Master ensures faster order processing, zero specification mismatch, accurate costing, seamless certification generation and complete traceability across manufacturing, quality, logistics and finance.
